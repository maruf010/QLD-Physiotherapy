Clinical Expertise with a Personal Touch

Founded by Melissa Murphy, Queensland Exercise Physiology (QEP) was established with a singular focus: to bridge the gap between complex clinical research and real-world, life-changing exercise interventions.

We don't believe in generic gym plans or trend-based workouts. As Accredited Exercise Physiologists (AEPs), we treat exercise as medicine. Every program we write is carefully tailored to support cardiovascular recovery, manage metabolic disorders, secure bone health against osteoporosis, and make healthy ageing a vibrant reality.

"Our body is capable of incredible adaptation at any age. When we apply precise, clinical-grade exercise loading, we don't just rehab an injury — we secure long-term independence."
— Melissa Murphy, Founder & Accredited Exercise Physiologist
