What to Expect

Your initial 60-minute clinical assessment is the foundation of your recovery. During this session, Melissa will:

    Comprehensive History: Review your medical files, pathology reports, surgeries, and goals.
  • 2
    Physical Assessment: Perform objective tests measuring strength, mobility, and balance.
  • 3
    Custom Programming: Design your tailored evidence-based exercise intervention framework.
