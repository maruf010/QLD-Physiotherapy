Meet Melissa Murphy

Accredited Exercise Physiologist (AESS, ESSAM)

Melissa is an Accredited Exercise Physiologist with a passion for helping people improve their health through movement. With experience across healthy ageing, chronic disease management, women's health, and strength training, Melissa works closely with each client to develop practical, achievable strategies that support long-term well-being.

She believes that exercise is a powerful medicine that should be accessible, enjoyable, and tailored to the individual's unique biological and lifestyle contexts.
